Who We Are

AutoWorld is a car dealership information platform designed to make the car buying process simple, clear and comfortable. Users can search cars by brand and price, check details, locate dealers and book test drives.

AutoWorld started off in collaboration with GOOD YEAR and later built its own independent dealership network with different showrooms for different brands at various city locations.

The website is built according to the project requirement where AutoWorld acts as an internet gateway to car dealership information.
